This top area shows your level goal and the menu. Read it first before you start building.

Drag blocks here. The glowing preview shows where your next block will snap.
The puzzle notches help blocks feel connected, so it is easier to place them in order.
Use Gamelan Beat to repeat actions. Blocks inside the loop will play again and again.
If something goes wrong, the wrong block will shake so you know what to fix.
